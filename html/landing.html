Mechanics

  • Users can place pixels on the canvas using their available "Bits"
  • Placing a pixel costs 1 Bit. Bits regenerate over time
  • Complete challenges to increase your bit capacity
  • During certain times, cooldown will be significantly reduced
  • Use your pixels to collaborate with others and create art together

House Rules

  • Keep It Respectful - Any inappropriate, offensive, or hateful imagery is strictly prohibited and will be removed by our moderators.
  • Teamwork is Encouraged - Collaborate with others to create large designs, but respect others' contributions.
